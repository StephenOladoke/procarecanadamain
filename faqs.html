What Payment Methods Are Available?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, electronic funds transfer, and direct billing options for your convenience. Our goal is to make the payment process seamless and secure.

Which Plan Is Right For Me?

Selecting the appropriate plan depends on your unique needs. Whether you're interested in Cavi Waiver, 245D services, or homecare, our team is here to guide you. Contact us for personalized assistance in determining the plan that best fits your requirements.

Do I have to commit to a contract?

We believe in flexibility. There is no mandatory commitment to a contract for our services. You have the freedom to choose the duration that suits you best, ensuring that you have control over your care plan.

What if I pick the wrong plan?

If you find that the initially selected plan doesn't align with your evolving needs, don't worry. We understand that circumstances change. Our team is here to assist you in adjusting your plan to better suit your current requirements.
