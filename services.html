Services Made With Care!

We understand that each of our clients has unique needs and preferences, which is why we offer customized care plans which we can tailor to meet those needs.

24-Hour Emergency Assistance

We can provide immediate support and assistance in the event of an emergency 24 hours a day. This service can help individuals and families feel safe and secure, knowing that help is available around the clock in case of any unexpected emergency.

Adult Companion Services

We designed this service to provide social and emotional support to adults who may be isolated or need companionship. These services can include activities such as conversation, board games, or outings and can help improve the overall well-being of the seniors.

Homemaker

We provide support to our clients and families by assisting with household tasks such as cleaning, laundry, meal preparation, and grocery shopping. We can help ease the burden of household responsibilities, allowing them to focus on other aspects of their lives.

Individual Community Living Support

This service provides people with disabilities or other support needs with the assistance they need to live independently in their own homes or in the community. This can include help with ADLs, as well as support with accessing community resources and social activities.

IHS With Training

We offer support to individuals with disabilities or other support needs, helping them to live independently in their own homes or the community. It involves assisting with ADL and training to help individuals develop skills and abilities.

IHS Without Training

We assist individuals with disabilities or other support needs to help them live independently in their homes or the community. It includes support with ADL, accessing community resources, and social activities, without any additional training or skill development.

Night Supervision

We provide support during the night, ensuring the seniors' safety and well-being while they sleep. This service can be especially beneficial for seniors who require assistance during the night, providing peace of mind for both the seniors and their loved ones.

Personal Support

This service provides assistance with ADLs, such as personal care, meal preparation, and transportation. This service is designed to help people with disabilities or other support needs maintain their independence and improve their overall quality of life.

Respite Care In-Home or Out-Of-Home

We provide temporary relief for caregivers by taking over their responsibilities for a short time, either in the individual's home or at a care facility. This service allows caregivers to take a break from their duties, reducing stress and promoting their overall well-being.

In-Home Family Support

We assist and support families who have a child with disabilities or other support needs. This service can include assistance with ADLs, access to community resources, and support with managing the child's needs, helping to improve the overall well-being of the family.

Independent Living Skills Training

We provide people with the training and support they need to develop the skills necessary for independent living. This can include assistance with managing finances, cooking, and other household tasks, and help with developing interpersonal and problem-solving skills.

Semi-Independent Living Skills

We provide clients an opportunity to live in a semi-independent setting while still receiving the support they need. This service can help them develop the skills and independence necessary for fully independent living while still having access to support and guidance.

Supported Living Services for Adult

We provide seniors with disabilities or other support needs with the assistance they need to live independently in their homes or the community. This service includes assistance with ADLs and helps with accessing community resources and social activities.
